Collaboration, to heal our nations

We Rise for Freedom, is the mother organisation of various projects that heal the mind, body, soul and our natural environment. We encourage collaboration, both within and outside of the organisation

Reconnect with the Magic of Nature

The Woodland WOOM Project regenerates land using permaculture methods. We create beautiful, wildlife friendly, food forests and landscapes, allowing visitors to reconnect with nature. Using various artistic, and technological mediums we provide engaging, immersive, educational experiences, with a strong focus on science and local history. We also provide various creative healing workshops and retreats

A World Aware

The Poetry Circle is based at The Woodland WOOM and provides community members with spaces to speak their truth and share their knowledge in a safe, creative and healing environment. We help to grow community awareness and heal mental health issues.

Healing through Art

Safe Space Studio is based at The Woodland WOOM. We help people suffering with mental and physical health issues, by providing creative healing workshops and exhibitions, that reconnect people with themselves, each other and the natural environment

Let food be your healer

For Freedom Wholefoods is based at The Woodland WOOM. We grow, source, supply and make, healthy, tasty, cruelty free food, for the local community of Essex
